About

THE ENGINEER
BEHIND THE CODE

My path into software started long before a keyboard. I began as a Project Engineer at Nibbi Bros in San Francisco — building a Low-Income Housing Development and an SFO Long-Term Parking Garage, running quantity takeoffs and coordinating across Civil, Architects, and Subcontractors.

From there I moved into survey and design — drafting As-Builts for SoCal Gas at Westland Group, where I automated Excel workflows in Visual Basic that cut thousands in company costs. That was the first time I realized software could make entire systems run better. I never forgot that.

Then two years at BKF Engineers in Redwood City as Lead AutoCAD Civil 3D Designer — grading, electric, signage, water, storm and sewer plans across high-value Bay Area projects including Treasure Island (SF), 2201 Valley St (Oakland), and Adrian Court (Burlingame). Working to Caltrans Standards, managing multiple projects simultaneously, always precise.

Since 2021 I've been building full-stack web applications and client sites through 100Devs. The tools changed. The discipline didn't.
